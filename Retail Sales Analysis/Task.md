# 📊 Power BI Case Study – RetailX Performance Challenge

---

## 🏢 Company Background

**RetailX** is a mid-sized Indian retail company that sells office supplies, furniture, and technology products. The company operates through both online and offline channels across four major regions: North, South, East, and West.

In recent months, RetailX has observed inconsistent growth across regions, fluctuations in profit margins, and increasing return rates. While the company collects large amounts of transactional data, it lacks a centralized system to monitor performance and drive strategic decisions.

---

## 🔍 Business Scenario

The leadership team at RetailX wants a clear, visual understanding of:

- How product categories and sub-categories are performing
- Which regions and customer segments are profitable or underperforming
- The impact of discounts on sales and profit margins
- Patterns and trends in returned products
- Year-wise and month-wise trends in sales and profitability

They’ve shared an Excel file containing:

- **Order data** with details like sales, profit, quantity, discount, and shipping
- **Return data** with IDs of orders that were returned
- **People data** with information on managers and their respective regions

---

## 🎯 Your Challenge

You have joined as a **Power BI Intern** at RetailX.

Your task is to explore the data and build a dashboard that helps management:

- Identify gaps and trends in business performance
- Highlight issues related to returns and discount policies
- Provide region-wise and product-wise business insights
- Support better decision-making through visuals and storytelling

> You have complete freedom to explore and present the data. There is no fixed methodology, no pre-defined metrics, and no required chart types. Your job is to think like an analyst, find the story in the data, and tell it visually.

---


## 📌 Note

Do **not** submit a presentation or written report. Only a self-explanatory, interactive Power BI file is expected.
